About Us


Welcome to Krishi Mitra, the vanguard of agricultural innovation, where we transcend the traditional farm-to-table experience by seamlessly uniting farmers with discerning consumers. In an era where transparency and fairness are paramount, Krishi Mitra stands as a beacon of equitable trade, empowering farmers to command the prices their hard-earned produce truly deserves—unfettered by middlemen and uncompromised by commercial interests.

Our platform offers more than just a marketplace; it’s an ecosystem designed to optimize every facet of the agricultural journey. With cutting-edge features like live market analytics, real-time meteorological insights, and personalized agronomic recommendations, Krishi Mitra equips farmers with the intelligence to make data-driven decisions, ensuring optimal harvests and sustained prosperity. For consumers, this means access to the freshest, purest, and most natural crops, meticulously cultivated and delivered with precision.

At Krishi Mitra, we don’t just facilitate transactions; we forge connections. Our commitment to additive-free, organically grown produce ensures that what you consume is as wholesome as it is flavorful, while our advanced logistical network guarantees that these farm-fresh goods reach your doorstep in their prime.

Join us in pioneering a new paradigm of agricultural excellence—where technology meets tradition, and where every purchase is a vote for sustainability, fairness, and quality. Krishi Mitra is more than an app; it’s a movement towards a more enlightened and equitable food system.

Experience the future of farming. Embrace the change. One crop, one connection, at a time.
